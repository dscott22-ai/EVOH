About This Project

This site was created for KSU's IT 3203 to explain the evolution of HTTP, its core concepts, and future directions.

Authors: Dymone Scott & Kyante Thompson

Goals

  • Present clear, student-friendly explanations of HTTP history and versions.
  • Demonstrate semantic HTML, external CSS, responsive layout, and accessible UI patterns.
  • Provide a dynamic quiz for self-assessment.
